1. Booking & Payment

  • To confirm a booking, a 5% advance of the total package price is required at the time of booking. This is paid online via Razorpay.
  • The remaining 95% balance is payable directly to us on the last day of your trip — UPI, bank transfer or cash.
  • All advance payments are processed by Razorpay using a secure HTTPS connection. We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net-banking and popular Indian wallets.
  • A booking is considered confirmed only after we send a written confirmation (email/WhatsApp) acknowledging receipt of the 5% advance.

2. Cancellation & Refund Policy

If you need to cancel a confirmed booking, please email us in writing at cancellation@andamanvoyages.in with your Booking Reference. The cancellation fee is calculated on the total trip cost and deducted from your 5% advance:

  • More than 60 days before travel: 95% refund of advance paid (only 5% cancellation fee retained).
  • 30 to 60 days before travel: 90% refund of advance paid (10% cancellation fee retained).
  • Less than 30 days before travel or no-show: 50% refund of advance paid (50% cancellation fee retained).

Worked example: For a ₹20,000 package, the advance is ₹1,000. If you cancel 65 days before travel you get ₹950 back; 45 days before you get ₹900; 20 days before you get ₹500.

Because the balance (95% of the trip cost) is paid only after travel, you are never charged the full amount upfront — there is no large balance to refund. The cancellation fee applies only to the 5% advance.

Note: Non-refundable third-party charges (flight tickets booked on your behalf, ferry bookings during peak season, hotel pre-payment penalties) are deducted in addition to the above.

Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method.

  • Airfare to and from Port Blair (unless explicitly bundled)

  • Personal expenses, tips, telephone bills, alcoholic beverages
  • Travel insurance
