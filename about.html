About Bharat Tours & Travels

Andaman holiday specialists since 2014 — 50,000+ happy travellers, 1,000+ local partners.

Who We Are

Andaman Voyages is the digital home of Bharat Tours and Travels, an India-based holiday-package specialist focused exclusively on the Andaman & Nicobar Islands. We design end-to-end trips to Port Blair, Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep), Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep), Baratang, Diglipur and Ross Island for honeymooners, families, friend groups and solo travellers alike.

Our company was founded in 2014 with a simple idea: take the friction out of planning an Andaman trip. Travellers used to spend weeks emailing five different hotels, three ferry operators and two transfer companies just to put a 7-day itinerary together. We do all of that for you — at prices the islands' locals would charge their own friends.

How We Work

Every package on this site is hand-curated by our team and a network of 1,000+ local guides, hotel partners, scuba instructors and ferry operators across the islands. We don't just resell — we negotiate directly with hotels in Havelock and Port Blair, hold blocks on the popular ferries (Makruzz, Nautika, Green Ocean), and run our own pickup vehicles in Port Blair. That's how we keep prices honest and itineraries flexible even during peak season (October to March).

Our office sits in Kolkata, West Bengal — strategically halfway between mainland Indian cities and the islands — with on-ground operations running daily out of Port Blair. The Kolkata team handles itinerary planning, hotel reservations and customer support; the Port Blair team handles airport pickups, ferry escorts, daily sightseeing and emergency response during your trip.

Where We Take You

Port Blair — the gateway. Cellular Jail, Corbyn's Cove, Ross Island, North Bay, Anthropological Museum.

Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep) — Asia's-best Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach snorkeling, Beach No. 7 sunset, world-class scuba diving at depths of 12–30 m.

Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) — Natural Bridge, Bharatpur and Laxmanpur Beaches, smaller and quieter than Havelock — perfect for couples seeking calm.

Baratang Island — limestone caves, mud volcanoes, mangrove creeks. A day-trip adventure for those who want something unusual.

Diglipur — North Andaman's quietest islands, Saddle Peak (highest point), turtle-nesting beaches. For repeat travellers who've already done Havelock-Neil.

Ross Island (Subhash Chandra Bose Dweep) — colonial ruins, deer & peacock walks, Light & Sound show, half-day trip from Port Blair.
