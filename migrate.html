Mirror Firestore Data Between Projects

Keep your secondary Firebase project (andaman-c85f0) as a hot backup of primary (andaman-b886d). Run this whenever you've made significant changes. If the primary's free quota gets exhausted, flip window.FIREBASE_USE to 'secondary' in js/firebase-config.js and your site is instantly live again on the backup with the latest mirrored data.

You must be logged in as admin in BOTH projects. Each Firebase project has its own Auth — your existing login in andaman-b886d does NOT carry over to andaman-c85f0. Use the buttons below to sign in.
