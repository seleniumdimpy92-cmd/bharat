Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

Bharat Tours and Travels ("we", "us", "Andaman Voyages") operates the website https://andamanvoyages.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or book a holiday package with us. By using the site you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to plan and confirm your trip:

  • Identity & contact details — full name, email, phone number, postal city — collected when you register, fill an enquiry form, or make a booking.
  • Booking details — travel dates, number of adults/children, package selected, and any preferences you share with us.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our payment processor Razorpay. We never see, store, or transmit your full card or UPI credentials. We receive only the transaction status and a non-sensitive Payment ID.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, referring URL — collected automatically by our web server and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bookings, send tickets/itineraries, and provide customer support.
  • To respond to enquiries and customise package recommendations.
  • To send you trip-related communications (booking confirmation, payment receipt, pre-travel reminders, cancellation/refund updates).
  • To send promotional emails about new packages or seasonal offers — only with your prior consent, and you can opt out anytime.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., GST invoicing, tax records, anti-fraud checks).

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use first-party cookies and may use third-party analytics (Google Analytics, etc.) to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These tools may set cookies to count visits, identify returning users, and measure conversions. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some site features may stop working.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following trusted vendors. Please review their own privacy policies:

  • Razorpay — payment processing (razorpay.com/privacy)
  • Google Firebase — authentication and booking storage (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (netlify.com/privacy)
  • Cloudflare — DNS, email routing, security (cloudflare.com/privacypolicy)
  • Google AdSense (if and when re-enabled) — advertising. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to our site or others. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only with parties strictly necessary to deliver your trip:

  • Hotels, resorts and ferry operators in Port Blair, Havelock and Neil Island — with the minimum data required for your reservation (name, dates, ID type/number where required by Andaman tourism rules).
  • Local transfer/cab operators — name and pickup details only.
  • Government authorities when legally compelled (court order, tax assessment, anti-money-laundering check).

We never sell your personal data to advertisers or marketing list vendors.

6. Data Retention

Booking and tax records are retained for at least 8 years as required by Indian tax law. Marketing-list email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Login accounts are retained until you ask us to delete them.

7. Your Rights

You can write to info@andamanvoyages.in at any time to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to legal retention)
  • Withdraw marketing consent
  • Lodge a complaint

8. Children

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Bookings for minors are made by their parent/guardian, who provides consent on the child's behalf.

9. Security

We use HTTPS encryption sitewide, secure cloud-hosted databases (Firebase) with strict access rules, and rely on PCI-DSS-compliant Razorpay for all payments. No transmission over the internet can be 100% secure, but we apply industry-standard safeguards.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be notified by email if you have an account.
